About

Join us to create Rainspiration (Rain & Inspiration) Ranch to transform a vacant lot in the city of Pasadena, into a green space that will excite the senses, invoke curiosity, encourage creativity, and share in the company of wildlife and others.  

The park features 3 rain gardens: A Pollinator Garden, Native Garden, and Fragrance Garden. The three gardens feature a distinct plant selection, yet all depend on the collection of local storm water and roof water from generous neighbors.

We also plan to build an outdoor classroom gazebo that can double as an event space. The gazebo hopes to be home to gardening, community meals, and cooking classes.  

The Ranch will include an edible garden suitable for 11 planter boxes worth of edible vegetation and the opportunity for visitors and neighbors to grow and enjoy fresh grown produce. Join us to create a beautiful space for all to share in Pasadena.
